Pay-On-Receipt (POR) Program

There are 15-20 generic manufacturers who routinely sell their products on secondary markets at discounted prices. These products are frequently denied credit by the manufacturer or credited at a fraction of their typical value.
If such product is returned to us it will only be processed under our Pay-On-Receipt reimbursement program and will be subject to a 20% additional fee. If the return is denied by the manufacturer, this product will be destroyed with no credit issued to the customer.

Return Eligibility

If upon review it is determined a return does not meet the necessary criteria for the timeframe of your choice, RSI reserves the right to change it to 90 days with a lower rate.
